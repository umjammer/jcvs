jCVS II

As you have undoubtedly deduced, jCVS II is the second incantation of jCVS, a Java CVS client.

JCVS implements a complete client for CVS, providing a convenient and intuitive GUI. Whether you are a CVS expert, or a new comer, jCVS will make CVS easier to use.

JCVS was authored by Tim Endres.
